Who I Am

I am a German composer and pianist. I was born on a cold December eve in 1770. I was initially taught by my father, Johann van Beethoven; after which I was taught by Christian Gottlob Neefe. My first work was published in 1783, where I was merely 13. When I turned 21, I moved to beautiful Vienna where I learnt a lot about music under the hand of Franz Joseph Haydn. It was after this that I started to gain fame, but I started to lose something on the way too: my hearing.

My Work

My work is commonly divided into three periods. The first period starts after my arrival in Vienna. The middle period starts after I started realizing that I was losing my hearing. My work here shows my heroism and my personal struggle. The late period begins with a study of older music and the greatest composers who ever lived.
